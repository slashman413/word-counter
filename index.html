About this free word & character counter

Whether you're writing an essay with a strict limit, an SEO meta description that must fit, or a social post, exact counts matter. This tool counts words, characters, sentences and estimated reading time live as you type — entirely in your browser.

How to use

  1. Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Watch the word, character and sentence counts update instantly.
  3. Use the reading-time estimate to judge how long your piece will take to read.

Why use it

Manual counting is slow and error-prone. Live counts help you hit limits for essays, meta descriptions (~155 characters), posts and abstracts without pasting into another app. Your text never leaves your browser, so it's safe for confidential drafts.

FAQ

How is reading time estimated?

From an average adult reading speed of roughly 200-230 words per minute.

Does the character count include spaces?

Yes — it reports total characters including spaces, which is what most platforms count.

Is my text sent anywhere?

No. All counting happens locally in your browser; nothing is uploaded or stored.
